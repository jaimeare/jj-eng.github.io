Intro

Arc Flash Studies at government facilities safeguard the lives of staff and visitors. An Arc Flash Study is more than a technical assessment it’s a proactive commitment to preventing catastrophic electrical incidents that could disrupt critical care operations. By identifying and mitigating arc‑flash hazards, we protect essential medical equipment, ensure uninterrupted facility operations, and most importantly, preserve the safety of those who serve our veterans.

This study also ensures full compliance with VHA Directive 1028, NEC Article 110.16, NFPA 70E, and OSHA standards — reducing liability, avoiding costly penalties, and demonstrating the VA’s leadership in workplace safety. Partnering on this effort means the VA can meet its mission with confidence, knowing its electrical systems are safe, compliant, and ready to support uninterrupted care.

About

At JJ Engineering, our mission is simple: deliver exceptional, compliant, and reliable solutions that protect people, assets, and operations. As a Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB), we bring the discipline, integrity, and mission focus of military service into every project we undertake.

Jorge Salazar — Founder, SDVOSB Owner & Project Manager A proud U.S. veteran, Jorge leads with the precision, accountability, and commitment forged through years of service. As Project Manager, he ensures every engagement is executed on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards. Jorge’s leadership bridges the gap between client vision and technical execution, making him the trusted point of contact for every project.

Jaime Arellano — Technical Director, Electrical Engineer (P.E.) With over 9 years of specialized experience in Arc Flash Studies, Jaime oversees all technical aspects of our electrical safety assessments. As a licensed Professional Engineer, he brings a rigorous, standards-based approach to every project, ensuring that each study is accurate, fully compliant with NEC, NFPA 70E, and OSHA regulations, and tailored to the unique needs of each facility. Jaime’s meticulous attention to detail guarantees that our deliverables not only meet regulatory requirements but also provide actionable insights to improve safety, reduce risk, and support long-term operational reliability.

Together, Jorge and Jaime form a leadership team that blends veteran led project management with deep technical expertise in arc flash hazard analysis. Whether serving federal agencies, healthcare facilities, or private sector clients, we are committed to delivering results that stand up to the highest levels of scrutiny — because lives, missions, and reputations depend on it.
